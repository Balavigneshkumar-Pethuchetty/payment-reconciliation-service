  1. You configure an email account (Gmail with App Password, or any IMAP) below.
  2. The background monitor polls it every N seconds for unseen emails.
  3. Emails are filtered by sender, subject keywords, and body keywords before parsing.
  4. Matching emails are sent to Ollama which extracts: amount, UPI ref, bank, status.
  5. A ParsedTransaction record is stored and an SSE event is pushed to your browser.
  6. The Live Feed card on the Create & Pay tab lights up → click Auto-Reconcile to match it.
⚠ For Gmail: use an App Password (not your regular password). Enable IMAP in Gmail Settings → Forwarding and POP/IMAP. Create App Password →
